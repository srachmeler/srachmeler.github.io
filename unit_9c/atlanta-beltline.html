The Atlanta BeltLine, the largest redevelopment project undertaken by the city, is still largely misunderstood.

Healthy

One key tenet of the Atlanta BeltLine's marketing collateral is the idea of health. The Atlanta BeltLine is making Atlanta healthy in a multitude of ways, from the physical to the economic, the transit to the environment. The colors are bright, the collateral vibrant and visually impactful.

Inclusive

A second tenet is the idea of inclusivity. The Atlanta BeltLine, when complete, will connect the City's 45 neighborhoods. All of the photos used in the marketing collateral are photos of actual residents from all walks of life using and enjoying the BeltLine.

Living

A final tenet in the marketing of the BeltLine is the idea of living in, on, around and with the BeltLine. The BeltLine is born of the city rather than imposed upon it.
